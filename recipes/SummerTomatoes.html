Summer Tomatoes

We live for ripe summer tomatoes.

There's no better way to eat them than tossed with lemon juice and fresh herbs to intensify their flavor.

Ingredients

  • Red wine vinegar
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Parsley sprigs
  • Mint leaves
  • Dill leaves
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Whisk together vinegar, lemon juice, and 1/2 tsp. each of kosher salt and pepper, then whisk in oil in a slow stream until well-blended.
  2. Gently toss tomatoes and herbs with vinaigrette. Season with salt and pepper.
