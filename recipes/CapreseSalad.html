Summer Tomatoes

A classic, crowd-pleasing appetizer, side dish, or light lunch that’s as refreshing as it is easy to prepare.

For this classic caprese salad recipe, it’s imperative that you use the freshest ingredients you can get your hands on: Thick slices of tomato (heirloom, please) at the peak of their season and which have never been refrigerated; milky fresh mozzarella cheese; fragrant basil leaves swiped from your backyard or your closest farmers market; and your best bottle of extra-virgin olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Vine-ripened tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzerella
  • Fresh basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Fine sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. WOn a large serving platter arrange tomato and mozzarella slices and basil leaves, alternating and overlapping them.
  2. GSprinkle salad with basil or arugula and oregano and drizzle with oil. Season salad with salt and pepper.
